TYRESE GIBSON IS FURIOUS EX-WIFE NORMA MITCHELL LEFT DAUGHTER WITH A FRIEND INSTEAD OF HIM

Tyrese Gibson is an angry dad after learning that his ex-wife, Norma Mitchell, left their daughter, Shayla Gibson, with a friend instead of letting him babysit. TMZ has the inside scoop.

According to the media outlet,

“Tyrese claims his ex, Norma Gibson, left the country for a few days in early January while their daughter, Shayla, was supposed to be in her custody. Tyrese says Norma pawned the kid off to a classmate for a one-night sleepover, while she jetted off to Trinidad.

Presumably, that means Norma left Shayla with a friend on her final day of custody … because Tyrese says Shayla was back under his care the following day.”

Tyrese recently demanded receipts that provided proof of his ex-wife needing a babysitter when Shayla is in her custody.

The celebrity father claimed that Norma’s expense for childcare stemmed from his ex-wife taking time out to visit the spa and enjoy personal trips sans their daughter. A judge later agreed that Tyrese should not be bound to Norma’s alleged expense of $25 per hour for babysitting if she could not show proof of employment that would lead her to need childcare.
