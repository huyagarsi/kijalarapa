What is Chip Z'Nuff's Net Worth?

Chip Z'Nuff is an American musician and singer who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Chip Z'Nuff was born in Chicago, Illinois in September 1968. He is best known for being the bassist for the rock band Enuff Z'Nuff. The band formed in 1984 and released their self-titled debut studio album in 1989 which was their only album to enter the Top 100 on the Billboard 200 chart.

Enuff Z'Nuff has also released the albums Strength in 1991, Animals with Human Intelligence in 1993, 1985 in 1994, Tweaked in 1995, Peach Fuzz in 1996, Seven in 1997, Paraphernalia in 1999, 10 in 2000, Welcome to Blue Island in 2003, ? in 2004, Dissonance in 2010, Clowns Lounge in 2016, and Diamond Boy in 2018. Chip Z'Nuff has also been a touring member of the band Adler's Appetite since 2005. The band has released the EPs Adler's Appetite in 2005 and Alive in 2012.
