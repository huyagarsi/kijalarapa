Villains Of Valley View is an American sitcom comedy television series that stars Isabella Pappas, Reed Horstmann, Malachi Barton, Lucy Davis and James Patrick Stuart. The series created by Chris Peterson and Bryan Moore centers on a family of super villains who must learn to live a normal life as civilians. Teenage villain Havoc defends her mother by standing up to the League Of Villains, forcing them to go on the run. For how long will they be able to hide their identities? This is the question that needs unraveling.

Villains Of Valley View Season 3 Cast: Who will appear?

The main cast of the show is Isabella Pappas as Army Maden (Havoc), Malachi Barton as Colby Madden (Platform), Reed Horstmann as Jake Madden (Chaos), Kayden Muller as Hartley, James Patrick Stuart as Vic Madden (Brainiac), and Lucy Davis as Eva Madden (Surge).

The recurring cast is Patricia Belcher, portrayed as Celia. Most of these casts are likely to be the same faces seen in season 2. The directors have not hinted at any additional cast or recasting of a character.

Villains Of Valley View Season 2 Recap:

The second season of The Villains of Valley View continues the story of the Maddens, a family of supervillains, as they navigate their undercover lives in Valley View following their victory over Onyx, the powerful leader of all villains. Throughout this season, the Maddens will encounter new obstacles and challenges that will test their abilities to protect their secret identities.

As the plot unfolds, the family will unearth exciting discoveries, unveil long-held family secrets, and experience unexpected emotions. Meanwhile, a formidable teenage villain plans her revenge against the Maddens, adding an additional layer of suspense and tension to the narrative.
