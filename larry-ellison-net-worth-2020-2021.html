Early Life

Larry Ellison was born in 1944 0n 17th August as Lawrence Joseph Ellison. His birthplace was Manhattan, New York, the United States of America. As if that was not enough, he got separated from his mother. He suffered from pneumonia when nine. His mother could not withstand that. Under those circumstances, his mother chose to leave her. His uncle and aunt were the people his mother left the adopting task.

He was in the second year when his adoptive mum passed on. Consequently, he left the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign without even sitting for his final exams. He pursued computer design for the one time that he stayed there. Therefore, Ellison, despite attending two universities, never graduated.
