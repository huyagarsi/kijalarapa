How does the challenge work in UNO?

If you suspect a Wild Draw 4 card has been played illegally (ex- the player has a matching card), then you may challenge them. They must show you their hand & if guilty, they must draw the 4 cards instead of you. If the challenged player is innocent, you must draw 6 total cards!

What is bluffing in UNO?

You can play a draw 4 wild at any time and force your opponent to draw 4 cards. However, if you do this when you have a card that can be played instead, either because it’s the same color as the top card on the discard pile, or is the same number/type, they can call your bluff and force you to draw instead.

What is wild draw 4 uno?

According to the official rules of UNO, “When you play this card (Wild Draw 4 Card), you get to choose the color that continues play PLUS the next player must draw 4 cards from the DRAW pile and lose their turn. … If turned up at the beginning of play, return this card to the deck and pick another card.”

What is rummy on the board?

Rummy is a group of matching-card games notable for similar gameplay based on matching cards of the same rank or sequence and same suit. The basic goal in any form of rummy is to build melds which can be either sets (three or four of a kind of the same rank) or runs (three or more sequential cards of the same suit).
