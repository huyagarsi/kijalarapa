Here's What It Is Like To Have Halsey Drive You Around!

The Without Me singer is a painter, a make-up artist, a singer, a song-writer and a writer who just published her book of poems, but it doesn't end there.

Halsey has confessed to being bullied on Twitter for not being able to drive for years now, so she finally decided to break her silence and enlighten her fans...and her bullies.

She's Done With The Driving Jokes

It's unclear when exactly the bullying began, but Halsey's tweet informed her followers that she actually does drive, has a license and has previously driven in Manhattan, New York City for years.

Her followers admitted that she was probably skilled at it, if she could drive through the busy streets of the Big Apple.

"I just choose not to anymore because it makes me anxious. Maybe I will drive again one day. Maybe I won't." Halsey wrote in her tweet.

"Thank you for joining “Stop Bullying Me I Know How To Drive” daily with Halsey!" she ended her tweet with a plea.

A fan replied and shared a video that dates back years ago, one that Halsey herself shared on her Keek account.

The video sees the singer driving, singing and dancing, saying that she might crash her car, and her fans were quite concerned about how she got her driving license after watching it.

"are you a good driver tho?" the fan wrote, alongside the video.

Halsey Is The A Fun Driver...And A DJ!

Her tweet was met with questions and some support from fans, but one user shared that the best part of not being able to drive, was to be seated in the passenger seat!

"It's always cooler to be the passenger and have control over the music".

Halsey jumped at the chance, and replied with a confession that she is an "A+ DJ!"

"My favorite thing to do. The mood and vibe is entirely in my hands. Time to shine," she wrote.

We have no doubts about the A+ singer, song-writer being an A+ DJ! Even though we can't actually experience it, it's fun to imagine what it would be like to have Halsey drive us around.
