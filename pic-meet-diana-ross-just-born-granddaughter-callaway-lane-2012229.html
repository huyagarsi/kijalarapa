PIC: Meet Diana Ross' Just-Born Granddaughter Callaway Lane!

Diana Ross has a sweet, sweet love hangover — for an adorable new granddaughter!

The legendary 68-year-old singer’s daughter, Chudney Ross, gave birth to a little girl named Callaway Lane in Los Angeles on Wednesday.

Chudney, 36, announced the happy arrival via Facebook with a sweet snapshot of her bundle of joy.

“We are so excited to welcome Callaway Lane to the world!,” wrote Chudney, who shares Callaway (her first child) with love Joshua Faulkner. “She was born on September 19, 2012 at 6:40pm and weighs 7 lbs 8 oz!”

Callaway is the second grandchild for Grammy winner Diana, whose eldest daughter Rhonda Ross Kendrick (Chudney’s sister), 41, welcomed son Raif-Henok in August 2009.

Married and divorced twice, the Supremes frontwoman has three additional children: Tracee Ellis Ross, 39, and sons Ross Arne Naess and Evan Olav Naess, both 24.
