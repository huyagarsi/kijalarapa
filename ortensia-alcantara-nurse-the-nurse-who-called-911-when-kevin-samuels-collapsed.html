Ortensia Alcantara Nurse: The Nurse Who Called 911 When Kevin Samuels Collapsed

Hortencia Alcantara was the woman who called 911 when Kevin Samuels collapsed.

She called the emergency response and identified the YouTuber to the authorities. She is not his nurse, against widespread speculation.

The duo reportedly spent the night together before Samuels started complaining of chest pain.

An overdose of cocaine was later found In the system of Samuels.

The Georgia police officials had responded to a call they received from an Atlanta apartment, on May 6, 2022.

The police entered the scene and “observed” fire department officials “performing CPR to a black male, unresponsive.”

The report revealed that officers “then met Ms. Ortencia Alcantara, who identified the male as Mr Kevin Samuels.”

“Ms Alcantara stated that she met Mr Samuels last night, came to his apartment, and spent the night with him.”

“Ms Alcantara also said that early morning, Mr Samuels complained of chest pain.”

“She attempted to help him, but he fell on top of her and proceeded to notify 911.”

The police report also stated that “Ms Alcantara also said she requested the 911 operator to contact the front desk for a defibrillator to keep Mr Samuels responsive until Grady arrived because she is a nurse.”

Kevin Samuels was then transported to Piedmont Hospital.

Samuels was a YouTube content creator and well-known on the platform with over 1.4 million subscribers.

He was considered a controversial personality due to his relationship advice and misogynistic comments towards black women.
