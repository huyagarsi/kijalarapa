What Happened To Adeed Fayaz?

Adeed and his brother-in-law said no. Then the seller pointed a gun at Fayaz’s head and demanded money. When the late officer said he had no money, the man pointed his gun at Fayaz’s brother-in-law.

That gave the officer a chance to break free of the headlock. The gunman then opened Fire, hitting Mr. Fayaz in the head, before fleeing and shooting both men.

According to Chief Essig, the brother-in-law could take a gun off Mr. Fayaz’s hip and Fired at least six shots at the seller as he ran.
