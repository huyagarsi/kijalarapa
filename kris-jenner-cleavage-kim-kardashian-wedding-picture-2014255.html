Kris Jenner Wears Cleavage-Baring Dress to Kim Kardashian's Wedding

All eyes may not have been on the bride at Kim Kardashian and Kanye West's wedding on Saturday, May 24 in Florence, Italy. The mother of the bride, momager Kris Jenner, certainly turned heads and showed some skin in her wedding day attire. 

The 58-year-old flashed extra cleavage in a form-fitted, long sleeve gown with a deep V-neck. Vogue Italia editor Franca Sozzani Instagrammed a picture with the reality star at the highly-anticipated nuptials.

"Beautifull evening at Fortezza Belvedere in Florence .. Fun with Kris … Kim mother!! @krisjenner #valentino#florence#wedding@kimkardashian," she captioned the picture.
