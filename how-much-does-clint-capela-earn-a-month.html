How Much Does Clint Capela Earn A Month?

Clint Capela is a Swiss professional basketball player for the National Basketball Association’s Atlanta Hawks. He is the highest-paid team athlete in Swiss history.

What is Clint Capela’s Net Worth?

Capela’s net worth is estimated to be $20 million as of July 2023, according to several estimates. This amazing amount stems from his great basketball career when he gained a substantial chunk of his value over time. Nike was one of Capela’s major sponsorship partnerships. Capela inked a multi-year contract with them in 2017, which includes him wearing their shoes during games and promoting their goods off-court.

Clint Capela’s Contract With Houston Rockets & Traded To Atlanta

Clint Capela agreed to terms with the Houston Rockets that will pay him a total of $90,000,000 over the course of 5 years, with a guaranteed sum of $80,000,000 and an annual average salary of $18,000,000. As part of a 4-team transaction, the following player was transferred from Houston to Atlanta (ATL): Denver (DEN) traded Malik Beasley, Juan Hernangomez, and Jarred Vanderbilt to Minnesota (MIN); Atlanta (ATL) traded Evan Turner and 2020 1st round pick to Minnesota (MIN).
