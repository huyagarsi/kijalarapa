Emily Ratajkowski Height: How Tall is the American Model?

Emily Ratajkowski is an American model, actress, and activist who has become one of the most recognized and sought-after figures in the fashion and entertainment industries.

Emily Ratajkowski Profile and Bio

Emily Ratajkowski was born on June 7, 1991, in London, England. She grew up in San Diego, California, and attended the University of California, Los Angeles, where she studied fine arts. However, she dropped out of college after her freshman year to pursue a career in modeling.

She rose to fame in 2013 with her appearance in Robin Thicke’s music video for “Blurred Lines,” and has since become one of the most recognizable models in the industry.
