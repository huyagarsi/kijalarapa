Lily-Rose Depp Confirms Relationship with Rapper 070 Shake on Instagram

Lily-Rose Depp and 070 Shake have made their relationship official on Instagram.

In a heartwarming gesture, the actress took to her Stories on May 12 to share a passionate kiss with the “Skin and Bones” rapper, whose real name is Danielle Balbuena.

Depp expressed her excitement over their four-month journey together, captioning the photo with “4 MONTHS WITH MY CRUSH.”Speculation about their romance started circulating during Paris Fashion Week in February when the couple was seen getting cozy.

This year will be quite eventful for Depp as she gears up to star in HBO’s The Idol alongside The Weeknd. The show, co-created by Reza Fahim and Euphoria mastermind Sam Levinson, will feature Depp as pop star Jocelyn.

Drawing inspiration from iconic pop divas like Britney Spears, Beyoncé, and Mariah Carey, Depp aims to portray a commanding and unapologetically sparkling character.

The series, also featuring Suzanna Son, Rachel Sennott, Troye Sivan, Dan Levy, Mike Dean, Moses Sumney, Da’Vine Joy Randolph, Hank Azaria, and BLACKPINK’s Jennie, is set to premiere on June 4 on HBO Max.

Before her relationship with 070 Shake, Lily-Rose Depp was romantically linked to French rapper Yassine Stein.

In November 2021, the pair were caught sharing a kiss while grabbing coffee in Los Angeles. Before that, she briefly dated Austin Butler in August 2021, and their PDA moments in London garnered attention.

However, perhaps her most significant relationship was with actor Timothée Chalamet. She shared a passionate moment on a yacht in September 2019 while working together on the film The King.

Following their breakup in April 2020, Chalamet candidly discussed his feelings about the viral yacht photos, expressing embarrassment and frustration.

However, he emphasized that the incident was not a publicity stunt but an intimate moment that unexpectedly made headlines.

Despite her high-profile relationships, Depp has always strived to keep her personal life private, valuing the importance of privacy instilled in her from an early age. The 23-year-old actress has carried this mindset into her career and remains focused on her craft.

With her official confirmation of her relationship with 070 Shake, Lily-Rose Depp embraces love again.

As the eldest child of Johnny Depp and Vanessa Paradis, she continues to make waves in the entertainment industry, capturing the hearts of fans with her talent and undeniable charm.
