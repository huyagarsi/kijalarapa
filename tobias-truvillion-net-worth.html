Tobias Truvillion (born October 1, 1975) is an African-American actor.Truvillion was born in Flushing, Queens in New York City. He attended Parson JHS. He has appeared on such television shows such as Law & Order: Special Victims Unit and in April 2006, joined the cast of the long-running soap opera One Life to Live as Vincent Jones. He is currently in production for the film, Blackout with Jeffrey Wright and Zoe Saldana, due for theatrical release in 2007. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
