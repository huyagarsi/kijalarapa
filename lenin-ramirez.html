Who is Lenin Ramirez:

Lenin Ramirez is a famous World Music Singer. He was born on January 24, 1989 and his birthplace is Culiacan, Mexico. Lenin is also well known as, Regional Mexican music recording artist and songwriter with a debut studio album titled Siempre Firme which placed on the Top Latin Albums Chart a year after its release in 2015. He followed it with Mi Puno y Letra (2016), Las Vueltas de la Vida (2017), and Bendecido (2018).

Lenin is originated from Mexico. He has written songs for Los Plebes Del Rancho and was signed by DEL Records like Jose Manuel Lopez Castro.
