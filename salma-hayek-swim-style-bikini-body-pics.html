Salma Hayek Swim Style, Bikini Body: Pics

Salma Hayek is almost always in a bikini — and she continuously leaves Us wanting more.

Hayek’s bathing suit collection includes everything from sexy animal print bikinis and neon sets to plunging one-pieces and cutout numbers.

In July 2023, Hayek celebrated hitting 25 million followers on Instagram with a steamy social media video. In the clip, she dove into a pool wearing a boho two-piece while twirling, splashing water around and stretching her legs against the walls before giving the camera a soft smile.

One of her most talked about looks came in 2019 when she flaunted her figure in a leopard print one-piece featuring a deep V-neck and cutouts on her sides. She teamed the getup with cat eye sunglasses and a voluminous hairdo.
