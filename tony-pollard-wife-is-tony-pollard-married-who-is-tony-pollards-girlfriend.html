Tony Pollard Wife: Is Tony Pollard Married? Who Is Tony Pollards Girlfriend?

Tony Randall Pollard is an American football running back for the Dallas Cowboys of the National Football League.

Tony Pollard attended Melrose High School, where he played high school football and as a senior, was a two-way starter at wide receiver and cornerback.

Tony Pollard accepted a football scholarship from the University of Memphis and as a sophomore, he posted 36 receptions for 536 yards (14.9-yard avg.) and 30 carries for 230 yards (7.7-yard avg.) and six touchdowns. Tony Pollard also led the nation with a school-record 40-yard average per kickoff return (22 for 881 yards) and four returns for touchdowns.

Tony Pollard was considered one of the best kickoff return specialists in college football, tying a FBS record with seven career kick-return touchdowns, 87 kickoff returns (second in school history), 2,616 kickoff return yards (second in school history), 30.1 kick-return average (school record) and 4,680 all-purpose yards (second in school history).
