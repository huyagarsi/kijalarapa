The UK-based restauranteur, whose establishments include London‘s Michelin-starred pub The Harwood Arms, The Elder in Bath and The Woodsman in Stratford-Upon-Avon, expressed concerns that people often leave the industry because they are left feeling extremely worn down from the long working hours.    ‘The most important thing we have in our restaurants is our staff,’ he said, speaking on the Biting Talk podcast.
