Lil Tjay Is An American Rapper

Tione Jayden Merritt, better known as Lil Tjay, is a rapper and vocalist from the United States. He rose to stardom in 2018 with his song “Resume,”. Later on,  the same year, he signed to Columbia Records.

The first letter of his initial name and the first three letters of his middle name are combined to form his stage name. True 2 Myself, Tjay’s debut studio album, was released in 2019.

After a year, he released the mixtape State of Emergency, which featured mainly New York City drill musicians. “True 2 Myself” debuted at number 5 on the Billboard 200. It became his first charted album to reach number 5.

Destined 2 Win, his second studio album, was released in 2021 which featured the track “Calling My Phone,”. Tjay had the collaboration with American singer, 6black.
