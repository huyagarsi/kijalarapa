No One Enjoyed the Cubs World Series Win More Than Bill Murray

At long last, Bill Murray can say he’s truly seen it all. The Cubs are World Series champions for the first time in 108 years! Of course, their unofficial team mascot was on hand to see his beloved team fly the W in Cleveland. And, boy, was he in rare form. You’ve seen him crash a White House presser to predict this win, sing for the home crowd, offer a fan a free ticket to the seat next to him, have a celebratory sing-along with Eddie Vedder, steal every post-game show, and, still, you ain’t seen nothing yet. Behold, scenes from Bill Murray’s greatest night ever.
