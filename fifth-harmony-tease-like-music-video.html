Fifth Harmony tease 'He Like That' music video

Fifth Harmony sure know how to spoil their fans. Not content with blessing the world with their third album tomorrow, the group will also be dropping a music video for ‘He Like That’.

Lauren, Normani, Dinah and Ally have shared a teaser for the video online, and it’s safe to say that these ladies are serving some serious looks and moves. The visual is directed by James Larese, and whilst the 15-second clip doesn’t give too much away, we can already tell that we’re in for a treat. The fierce foursome are seen showing off their sultry looks, before kicking into choreography at the beginning of the catchy chorus.

The Fifth Harmony girls marked their new era of music with ‘Down’ featuring Gucci Mane, before unveiling album track ‘Angel’. With each track, they’ve shown a development in their sound, proving that they’re stronger than ever before. We’ve no doubt that ‘He Like That’ is going to a standout from their third album.

To celebrate the release of their self-titled album, Fifth Harmony will be doing a Vevo Live, becoming the first band to ever do one. The Vevo Live will give fans the opportunity to tune into a live video chat on Vevo, allowing fans from all over the world to get involved. The music video for ‘He Like That’ will also be premiered during the chat.

Fifth Harmony’s Vevo Live will take place on Friday August 25 at 11:45pm BST, live from LA. Register your email address here in order to RSVP to the event.
