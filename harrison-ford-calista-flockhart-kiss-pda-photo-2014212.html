Harrison Ford, Calista Flockhart Kiss: PDA Photo

You guys! Harrison Ford was photographed planting a passionate smooch on his wife Calista Flockhart this past weekend in L.A.

The couple had landed via private jet in the city when Ford, 72, grabbed his wife and leaned in for a kiss.

Flockhart, 50, wore no makeup and stood on the tips of her toes to match lips with her tall husband. Like Ford, she was dressed casually in a pair of jeans, keeping warm in a puffy jacket. Ford, meanwhile, rocked shades, a plaid shirt, and a tiny stud earring in his left lobe.

The stars and their 13-year-old son Liam were returning home after celebrating Thanksgiving in Jackson Hole, Wyo. (Meanwhile, Ford's grown chef son Ben Ford offered Us Weekly Thanksgiving prep tips last week.)

Despite their rather large age difference, Ford has previously expressed how thankful he is for Flockhart. "I was 60 when I fell in love with Calista," he told Hello! magazine in 2009. "What surprised me most was that I was still capable of establishing a long-lasting relationship, making a serious commitment. With my children, in my private life or my work, I try to learn from my mistakes and evolve."

Most recently, the Ford household has been preparing for the actor's return to the big screen. Ford will reprise his role as Han Solo in the upcoming J.J. Abrams-directed Star Wars installment, The Force Awakens, and stars opposite Blake Lively in the 2015 romantic drama, The Age of Adaline.
