Cacee Cobb News - Us Weekly

Name:Cacee Cobb

Birthday:December 2, 1977

About

As a former personal assistant to Jessica Simpson, Cacee Cobb has made a name for herself in Hollywood.

Born in 1977 in Texas, Cobb has appeared on the small screen on reality shows centered around the rich-and-famous life of Simpson, including Jessica Simpson: A Public Affair and Newlyweds: Nick & Jessica.

Cobb and her husband, Donald Faison, are the parents of son Rocco, born in 2013 and daughter Wilder, born in 2015.
