NATALIE NUNN ON NURSING: BREASTFEEDING IS NOT EASY AT ALL

Natalie Nunn and Jacob Payne are enjoying parenthood. The new celebrity mom recently shared photos and her experience thus far as a parent with fans.

“My husband has been over the moon about our daughter,” Natalie exclaimed. The reality star also shared a candid of Jacob looking at little Journey Ruth as she rested in the carrier. “I’m so blessed to have the both of you,” Nunn wrote. The celebrity mom went on to tell her husband to “keep up the great daddy duties.”

https://www.instagram.com/p/BTjwIkhhfXx/?taken-by=realmissnatalienunn&hl=en

Natalie shared her struggle with nursing her daughter a few days ago. “Breastfeeding is not easy at all,” the star mom told fans. “Since the day she was born I felt like at the hospital from the doctors to the pediatrician and lactation consultant they’ve been a little discouraging and pushy! Telling me things I’m doing wrong and I shouldn’t be doing this or doing that,” Nunn explained.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BTcPv9IBJVf/?hl=en

The celebrity went on to give fellow new moms advice. “Don’t beat yourself up over breastfeeding and pumping,” Natalie admonished. “Don’t let anyone make you feel bad about how you decide to do things and what’s best for you and your baby!”

Little Journey Ruth Payne is Natalie Nunn and Jacob Payne’s only child. She was born last month.
